What is this project?

As the name suggests, we (the University of Akron Amateur Radio Club), will be launching a 1200g balloon filled with hydrogen, into the sky.

Through the use of amateur radio technologies and the Global Positioning System (GPS), we hope to retrieve the payload at the end of the event.

Who is behind it?

A group of friends who belong to the University of Akron Amateur Radio club are behind the High Altitude Balloon project.

Stay in the know.

We will be live-streaming video of our launch as well as updating Twitter with status information. We will also be sending our telemetry data to a website we have created called MapView, which allows you to track the path of the balloon from anywhere you have an internet connection.
