--- title: Welcome ---

Launch failed. :-(

Due to high winds and poorly placed trees, the launch failed. Upon release, the balloon drifted and rose, but did not clear a tree. The payload snagged and got caught in the tree.

We appreciate all of your support, interest and encouragement. Please join us on our next launch.

- The W8UPD HAB Team

What was this project?

As the name suggests, we (the University of Akron Amateur Radio Club), attempted to launch a 1200g balloon filled with hydrogen, into the sky.

Through the use of amateur radio technologies and the Global Positioning System (GPS), we hoped to retrieve the payload at the end of the event.

Who was behind it?

A group of friends who belong to the University of Akron Amateur Radio club was behind the High Altitude Balloon project.

Stay in the know.

We live-streamed video of our launch and updated Twitter with status information. We will also sent our telemetry data to a website we have created, called MapView, which allowed you to track the path of the balloon from anywhere you had an internet connection.
